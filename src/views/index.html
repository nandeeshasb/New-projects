About Us

Our professional approach to recruitment and manpower customization solutions has led our company to catch attention of more than 90 leading Corporate, Public and other employing audiences from 11-odd industries that include Oil & Gas, construction, IT, Service Industry, Telecom, Education, Facility management, Retail, Automobiles, Office Automation, and Logistics, among others. Since the inception of our staffing consulting services in 2014, TAZ Job Consultancy is being able to fulfill the manpower needs of those industries quite satisfactorily, and that's why our strategic partners have laid their deep-rooted trust on our scruples and systems regarding Smarter Staffing process.
One of the key goals for the TAZ team is to establish a Human Resources infrastructure that supports employers, owners and managers in their current business and the future growth requirements. TAZ works in partnership with business owners and managers to build a value adding workforce by implementing a practical performance management system that effectively tracks the performance of individual employees. Working hand in hand from bottom to the top management of TAZ serves as the based-on quality, excellence, professionalism and trustworthiness that will satisfy the needs and requirements of each clients and customers.
TAZ ensures that each services impacts technologically, socially and environmentally driven to each projects align with the QATAR National Vision 2030.
